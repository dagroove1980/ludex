## Product Overview

**Ludex** is an AI-powered board game rulebook companion. Users upload PDF rulebooks, and the app uses AI to extract, organize, and make rules searchable through an interactive interface with an AI chat assistant.

**Tagline**: "Turn PDFs into Play"

---

## What Makes Ludex Special?

1. **AI-Powered**: Automatically organizes rulebooks into sections, strategy tips, and quick-start guides
2. **Interactive Chat**: Ask questions about game rules and get instant AI-powered answers
3. **Personal Library**: Build your own digital collection of board game rules
4. **Smart Processing**: Upload once, AI processes automatically in minutes

---

## Design Theme: Fantasy & Board Games

Transform Ludex from a functional tool into a **magical gaming grimoire** that feels like opening a beautifully illustrated rulebook from a premium fantasy board game.

### Visual Identity

**Mood**: Mystery, discovery, adventure, craftsmanship, wisdom

**Color Palette**:
- **Deep Purple** (`#6B46C1`): Primary - magic, mystery, premium
- **Rich Gold** (`#D97706`): Accents - treasure, achievements, CTAs
- **Forest Green** (`#059669`): Success states, growth
- **Crimson Red** (`#DC2626`): Errors, alerts
- **Parchment/Cream** (`#FEF3C7`): Backgrounds, cards
- **Charcoal** (`#1F2937`): Text, depth

**Typography**:
- **Headers**: Elegant serif (`Cinzel`, `Playfair Display`, or `Bodoni Moda`)
- **Body**: Modern sans-serif (`Inter`, `Poppins`, or `Work Sans`)

**Style**: 
- Fantasy/medieval aesthetic
- Premium board game feel (like Gloomhaven, Scythe rulebooks)
- Parchment textures, gold accents
- Decorative borders and elements

## Component Specifications
